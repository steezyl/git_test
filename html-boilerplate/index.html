Ingredients

  • 200g Gratted Carrot
  • 600g Chicken Mince
  • 1tbs Veg Oil
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • 2tbs Chicken Stock
  • 1 egg yolk (seperate and keep white)
  • 1tbsp Garlic Powder
  • Spring Roll Wrapers

Method

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Cut spring roll pasty in half diagonally
  3. Get some mix and lay it across the base of the triangle
  4. Dab some egg white on the top of the triangle and wrap
  5. Deep fry in vegetable oil
